Academic Programs

Explore our diverse range of courses designed to equip you with practical skills and in-depth knowledge for today's competitive world

Basic Education (K to 12)

Our K to 12 program lays a strong foundation for lifelong learning, focusing on academic excellence and character development. We provide a well-rounded curriculum that nurtures intellectual, emotional, and social growth. From kindergarten to senior high school, students are guided to become confident and competent individuals.

Colleges

Our college programs are designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills and critical thinking abilities. With a strong emphasis on hands-on learning and professional development, we prepare graduates for success in their chosen careers. Each course is led by experienced faculty committed to academic excellence and student growth.

Gradute Studies

Our graduate programs offer advanced academic and professional training for individuals seeking to deepen their expertise. Whether you're pursuing a master’s degree for career advancement or personal fulfillment, we provide rigorous and research-driven instruction. Join a community of scholars committed to innovation, leadership, and impact.

Our Facilities

Discover the modern facilities that support our students' learning, growth, and well-being.

St. Agustine Library

Our library provides a wide range of academic resources, from books and journals to digital databases. It’s a quiet, comfortable space for research, learning, and personal growth.

Podium

Our podium serves as a central space for events, presentations, and student activities. It's designed to foster engagement, expression, and a sense of community.

Thagaste Miniforest

The miniforest offers a peaceful green space on campus where students can relax, reflect, or study in a natural setting. It promotes well-being and environmental awareness within an academic environment.
